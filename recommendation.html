Recommendations To Increase Sales by 10-15% in Next 12 Months

Product Stock Optimization

1. Food

Seasonal Stock:
Increase stock during the hot months (June-August) as sales increase.


Bundling Promotion:
Offer discounts on joint food and beverage purchases to boost sales.

2. Carbonated Beverages

Routinely Update Stock:
Keep stock of food products up-to-date as sales are highest throughout the year, running out of stock can reduce revenue significantly.


Product Diversification:
Add a variety of snacks, including healthy options such as granola bars or dried fruits.

3. Non-carbonated Beverages and Water:

Fixed Stock:
Maintain stock as sales are consistent throughout the year.


Strategic Placement:
Place products in highly visible locations to attract consumer attention.

4. Others Category:

Product Evaluation:
Conduct periodic evaluations to determine which products should be removed or added.


Promotions and Discounts:
Use promotions and discounts to increase sales of less popular products.
